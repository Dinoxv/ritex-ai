## Nguyen tac

UI cua Hyperscalper khong duoc thiet ke de dep.
UI duoc thiet ke de trader ra quyet dinh dung va nhanh.

## Design pillars

- Clarity over decoration
- Signal over noise
- Risk visibility over visual effects

## Color system

3 tang bat buoc:
- Structure layer (background, grid, separators)
- Signal layer (entry, trend, alerts)
- Risk layer (SL, liquidation risk, warning states)

Rules:
- Bullish/Bearish phai nhat quan toan app.
- Warning/Error khong duoc trung semantic voi signal trade.
- Contrast du de scan trong dieu kien chart dong nhanh.

## Typography va data density

- Price, PnL, signal labels la cap uu tien cao nhat.
- Font size va weight phai toi uu cho scan theo cot/dong.
- Tranh chu trang tri lam mat focus.

## Motion

- Chi dung motion de bieu dat state change.
- Pending/confirmed/rejected phai khac biet ro.
- Cam animation lap vo nghia gay met mat.

## Rule toi thuong

Moi thay doi giao dien phai tra loi duoc cau hoi:
Co giam do tre nhan thuc cua trader hay khong?
