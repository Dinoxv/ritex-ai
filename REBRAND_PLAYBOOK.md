## Messaging framework (Pain -> Mechanism -> Outcome)

Pain:
- Retail chay cham hon thi truong vi quy trinh thu cong va manh mun.

Mechanism:
- Exchange abstraction + Trend Matrix + confluence scoring + risk engine.

Outcome:
- Rut ngan chu ky y tuong -> backtest -> live.
- Tang ky luat va tinh nhat quan khi thi truong bien dong manh.
